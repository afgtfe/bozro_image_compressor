Purpose: This is used to hold images during the compression process or before you choose to save or share them.

Management: These cached files are temporary and are managed by your operating system. They are usually cleared when the app is closed or storage is needed.

Saving & Sharing: When you save or share a compressed image, it is written directly to your device's storage or passed securely to other apps (like WhatsApp, Gmail, etc.) via the standard Android System Sharing Intent.
