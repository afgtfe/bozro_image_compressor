•
Personal Data: The developer of this app does not collect, store, or share any of your private personal information, such as your name, phone number, email address, or precise location.

Third-Party Services (AdMob): To keep this app free, we use Google AdMob for advertising. 3. Image Processing and Data Security
•
On-Device Processing: All image compression and optimization are performed locally on your device. Your images are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party cloud services.
•
Saving Images: When you save a compressed image, it is written directly to your device's storage. You have full control over these files.
•
Sharing Images: If you use the "Share" or "Save All" feature, the app uses the standard Android System Sharing Intent. Your images are securely passed to the apps you choose (like WhatsApp, Gmail, etc.) for sharing.

4. Permissions and Their Necessity To provide its features, the app requires the following permissions:
•
Camera: Used to capture new photos within the app for immediate compression.
•
Storage (Read/Write/Media Access): Used to select existing images from your gallery for compression and to save the final compressed images back to your device.
•
Internet: Used solely to load advertisements from Google AdMob and to allow you to visit external links (like "Rate Us" or our website).
5. Children’s Privacy Our app is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected by the developer, we are fully compliant with children's privacy regulations.
